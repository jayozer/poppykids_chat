Q: Why is it important to treat Baby Bottle Tooth Decay?
A: Treating this condition is crucial because baby teeth act as placeholders for permanent teeth. Losing baby teeth prematurely can affect the spacing and alignment of permanent teeth, impacting overall oral health and requiring future orthodontic work.

Q: What are the benefits of choosing a pediatric dentist for your child?
A: Pediatric dentists specialize in treating young children and use child-friendly equipment and decor to make dental visits less intimidating and more enjoyable, encouraging regular dental checkups.
